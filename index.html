Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2025

White Dove Cleaning Services ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

When you submit our estimate request form, we collect your first name, last name, phone number, email address, and any additional details you provide. We also collect anonymous usage data (pages visited, time on site) through Google Analytics if you consent to cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We use your contact information solely to respond to your estimate request and provide cleaning services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use Formsubmit.co to process form submissions (delivers your request to our email), and Google Tag Manager/Analytics for anonymous site analytics (only with your consent). These services have their own privacy policies.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies only when you accept them via our consent banner. Declining cookies disables all analytics tracking. Essential site functionality works without cookies.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to fulfill your service request. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Indiana residents and EU visitors have additional rights under applicable privacy laws. Contact us at whitedovecleaningco@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at whitedovecleaningco@gmail.com or call (317) 793-2172.
